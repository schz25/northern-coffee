Welcome to Northern Coffee Co. where we're passionate about bringing you the best coffee experience possible. Our coffee is sourced from the finest beans around the world and roasted locally in California, ensuring the freshest and most flavorful cup every time.

Above Average

We believe that coffee is an art form, and our skilled baristas take pride in crafting each cup to perfection. Whether you're in the mood for a classic latte or a specialty drink like our signature cold brew, we've got you covered. We also offer a variety of non-coffee options like teas and smoothies for those looking for something different.

Our Ethics

At our California coffee company, we're committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing practices. We work closely with our farmers to ensure fair wages and environmentally responsible practices, and we use eco-friendly materials whenever possible.

Our Vibe

Our cozy and inviting cafes are the perfect place to relax, catch up with friends, or get some work done. We strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Our Collection

Our Signature Coldbrew

Unlike traditional hot coffee, our cold brew is never exposed to heat, which means it's less acidic and less bitter. The result is a refreshing and easy-to-drink coffee that's perfect for those who prefer a milder taste. Our cold brew is also lower in acidity, making it easier on the stomach and more gentle on tooth enamel.
